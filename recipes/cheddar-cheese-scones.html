Cheddar Cheese Scones

Description

Light, flaky, cheesy, and just plain delicious scones!

  • Prep: 15 mins
  • Cook: 15 mins
  • Total: 30 mins
  • Servings: 12
  • Yield: 12 scones

Ingredients

  • 4 cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 1/4 cup White Cane Sugar
  • 2 tbsp Baking Powder
  • 2 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 tsp Baking Soda
  • 2 cups Shredded Cheddar Cheese
  • 8 oz Sour Cream
  • 1/2 cup Canola Oil
  • 1/3 cup Whole Milk
  • 2 eggs

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400Â°F and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Whisk togehter all of the dry ingredients.
  3. Fold the Shredded Cheedar Cheese into the dry ingredients.
  4. Whisk the wet ingredients together and then add to the dry ingredients.
  5. Stir until just moistened.
  6. Knead dough on a lightly floured surface 10 to 12 times. Pat out to a thickness of 3/4 inch. Cut into 3-inch squares or cut circles with a 3-inch cutter. Transfer to the prepared baking sheet.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven until light golden brown, about 15 minutes.
